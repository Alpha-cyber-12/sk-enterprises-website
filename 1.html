About Us

Established over 20 years ago, S.K. Enterprises is a fully registered and trusted manufacturer based in the Jhilmil Industrial Area, Delhi, specializing in high-precision components like Electronic Base Shells and robust MS Rollers.

Our legacy, driven by Proprietor "Mr. Vikas Agarwal", is built on a commitment to high precision skills, ensuring every product meets the strictest standards for dimensional accuracy and quality. This dedication gives our clients the confidence that comes from partnering with a reliable and experienced industry leader.

Core Specialization

Specialized Cylindrical Components

We are a dedicated manufacturer for the 'Electronic Printing' and 'Rotogravure Industries', providing critical, dimensionally accurate components that are essential for high-speed, flawless production processes. Our focus is narrow, but our precision is absolute.
